🎓 EduGuard TN

AI Early Warning System for Student Dropout Prevention — Transforming reactive reporting into proactive prevention.

🎯 Our Mission

EduGuard is designed to shift education systems from reactive dropout reporting to proactive dropout prevention. Using cutting-edge machine learning and explainable AI, we give teachers and education officers the tools they need to identify struggling students before they leave school, and provide timely, evidence-based support.

🤖 How the AI Works
1
Data Collection
Attendance, marks, distance, family background are collected via CSV upload.
2
ML Prediction
Gradient Boosting model computes a risk score from 0-100 for each student.
3
Explainable AI
The system shows the top contributing factors behind each student's score.
4
Intervention & Tracking
Teachers apply interventions and track outcomes for 30 days.
✨ Platform Features
🤖

AI Risk Prediction

Gradient Boosting ML model trained on 10 student features to predict dropout risk (0-100).

🔍

Explainable AI (XAI)

Clear explanations of why a student is flagged, not just a black-box score.

💡

Intervention Engine

Evidence-based recommendations matching each student's specific risk factors.

💬

Parent Messages

Auto-generated supportive messages in English and Hindi for WhatsApp/SMS.

🗺️

District Heatmap

Visual analytics for education officers to allocate district resources effectively.

📈

Outcome Tracking

30-day progress tracking after interventions with attendance and marks charts.

🏛️

Gov. Scheme Matcher

Automatically match students with eligible government scholarship and support schemes.

🚨

Early Alert System

Automatic notifications when a student's risk score crosses the 70-point threshold.
